Operation Glasshouse

Session 1: "Static"

Briefing

You are a SOC team responding to an evolving cybersecurity incident at XYZ Pay, a Buy Now Pay Later fintech company in Adelaide. It's Monday morning, 14 October 2024.

Rules

  • Your team has 12 action tokens — spend them to investigate, escalate, or act
  • Some actions cost tokens. Choose wisely — you can't get them back
  • Some envelopes open automatically at set times — watch the clock
  • When escalating to Priya (SOC Manager), complete the Self-Assessment first
  • At session end, you should have enough information to complete a Triage Report

Roles (if playing as a team)

  • Alert Analyst — SIEM alerts, log analysis
  • Ticket Handler — Service tickets, employee comms
  • Intel Analyst — Threat intel, IOC research
  • Incident Coordinator — Timeline, documentation
  • Escalation Lead — NPC communication
  • Infrastructure Liaison — Technical actions
